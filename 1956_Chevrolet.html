1956 Chevrolet

"Loves to Go ... And Looks It!"

Introduction

Usually ignored in the family, the 1956 Chevy is like the middle child of the Tri Fives. However it did receive a new full-width front bumper & it’s 2-3 inches longer than the ‘55. It has a new wheel opening design and a hidden fuel cap behind the driver side tail light.

Engines

Transmissions: 3-SP = 3-speed, OD = Overdrive, PG = Powerglide

  • Straight Six 235cid, 140hp -- 3-SP, OD or PG
  • V8 265cid, 162hp -- 3-SP or OD
  • V8 265cid, 170 -- PG
  • V8 265cid, 205hp -- 3-SP, OD or PG
  • V8 265cid, 255hp -- 3-SP, OD or PG
