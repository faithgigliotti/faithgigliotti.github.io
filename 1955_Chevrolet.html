1955 Chevrolet

"New Look! New Life! New Everyhing!"

Introduction

Starting off the Tri Five series with a stylized-jet hood ornament, is the 1955 Chevy. The first Chevy to have a production V8 engine, a 265 cubic inch small block.

Engines

Transmissions: 3-SP = 3-speed, OD = Overdrive, PG = Powerglide

  • Straight Six 235cid, 123hp -- 3-SP or OD
  • Straight Six 235cid, 136hp -- PG
  • V8 265cid, 162hp -- 3-SP, OD or PG
  • V8 265cid, 180hp -- 3-SP, OD or PG
