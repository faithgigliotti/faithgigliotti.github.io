1957 Chevrolet

"Sweet, Smooth and Sassy!"

Introduction: 

Finishing the Tri Five series is the faster and flashier 1957 Chevy. It has a whole new design and only shares the roof, doors and trunk in common with the previous years. The most popular year of the three, famous for its tail fin and side body flash. A 283ci V8 engine that can have a mechanical fuel injection.

Engines 

Transmissions: 3-SP = 3-speed, OD = Overdrive, PG = Powerglide, TG = Turboglide

  • Straight Six 235cid, 140hp -- 3-SP, OD or PG
  • V8 265cid, 162hp -- 3-SP or OD
  • V8 283cid, 185 -- PG or TG
  • V8 283cid, 220hp -- 3-SP, OD, PG or TG
  • V8 283cid, 245hp -- 3-SP, PG or TG
  • V8 283cid, 250hp -- 3-SP, PG or TG
  • V8 283cid, 270hp -- 3-SP
  • V8 283cid, 283hp -- 3-SP (Fuel Injection)
