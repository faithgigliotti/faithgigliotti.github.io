Introduction

The Tri Five Chevys are one of the most classic and beloved American cars. This term Tri five is a nickname to represent the specific set of 1950s Chevys. The three years of 1955, 56 and 57. Each year has three model classes. The base economical One Fifty, the middle class elegant Two Ten and the top class luxurious Bel Air.

About Me
